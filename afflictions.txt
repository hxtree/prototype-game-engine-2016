Sacrifice  when Spirit reaches 0 Spirit Points are taken from Life. This is the only way that some characters will have enough Spirit Points to perform certain actions
Berserk – character can only attack and Power increased by Drive
Lifeless – characters stats are reduced to 0 and character cannot be healed
Knocked Out – character is unable to move. Set when Stamina is less than 0
Confused – character is unaware of surrondings
Drunk – character accuracy decreases
Doomed – character has a countdown timer placed over their head which must removed before it runs out or they become Lifelsss
Mortally Wounded – health rapidly decreases
Frozen – character is incased in block of ice after a certain amount of rounds they become Lifeless
Cold – (stackable) speed reduced by 10%
Burned – does damage when moving
Sleepy – speed decreased skip random turns
Down – character is disabled and has fallen down
Disarmed – character must get weapon and arm self again in order to attack
Bound – character cannot perform any actions but to Break Free (interaction) or use Tool
Blitz – (stackable) character Strength increases and Speed decreases while in combat (-5x through +5x)
Exiled – character is kick out of battle cannot return until battle is over
Infection – (stackable) character is damage each turn until infliction is healed
Metamorphic – character is inflicted with random status affect each turn
Omni – character enters a berserk like mode and attacks uncontrollable
Fear – character is too affraid to move
Stupid – character Intelligence is decreased and character unable to use Learn
Zipped – character cannot use Gear
Silence – character cannot use Spells
Fathom – character cannot use Drive
